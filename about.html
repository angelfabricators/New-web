About Us

WHO WE ARE

Engineering Excellence Since 1985

Angel Fabricators Pvt Co Ltd was officially established in 1985 and has built a strong reputation for delivering reliable engineering, fabrication, and industrial maintenance solutions. We have successfully worked with leading organizations across Tanzania, Kenya, and Zimbabwe.

With over 40 years of experience, our company continues to provide innovative engineering solutions while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide world-class engineering, fabrication and industrial maintenance services through innovation, professionalism and quality workmanship. We are committed to creating employment opportunities for young people, developing technical skills, supporting industrial growth and delivering value-driven solutions that exceed customer expectations across Africa.

Our Vision

To become one of Africa's leading engineering and fabrication companies, recognized for excellence, innovation, safety and sustainable engineering solutions that create lasting value for clients, communities and future generations.
