WHO WE ARE

Engineering Excellence Since 1985

Angel Fabricators Pvt Co Ltd was officially established in 1985 and has built a strong reputation for delivering reliable engineering, fabrication, and industrial maintenance solutions. We have successfully worked with leading organizations across Tanzania, Kenya, and Zimbabwe.

With over 40 years of experience, our company continues to provide innovative engineering solutions while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.
